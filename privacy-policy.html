Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines Midwestern Construction's practices with respect to information collected from users who access our website at midwestern.construction ("the Site"). By using the Site, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Grounds for Data Collection

We process personal information to fulfill our contractual obligations to you, to protect our legitimate business interests, and to comply with applicable legal requirements. By using our Site, you consent to our collection and use of your information as described here.

Types of Information We Collect

Non-Personal Information: We collect aggregated usage data and technical information including browser type, operating system, language preferences, and activity tracking such as pages viewed, clicks, and actions taken on the Site.

Personal Information: We may collect the following personal information:

  • Device Information: Geolocation data, IP address, and unique device identifiers
  • Registration Information: Full name, email address, and physical address when voluntarily submitted

Information Sources

Data is collected from:

  • Voluntary submissions by users (contact forms, estimate requests)
  • Site usage and browsing behavior
  • Third-party providers including analytics vendors

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Communications and service updates
  • Responding to estimate requests and inquiries
  • Marketing and promotional materials (with opt-out available)
  • Statistical analysis to improve our services

Information may be shared with subsidiaries, affiliated companies, and trusted third parties for hosting, service delivery, marketing, and analytics purposes.

Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your information when required to comply with applicable law, enforce our policies, prevent fraud, defend legal claims, or protect the safety and rights of our users and the public.

Your Rights

You may request confirmation of how we process your data, access copies of your information, rectification of inaccurate data, erasure of your data, or restrictions on processing. These rights are not absolute and may be subject to our legitimate interests. To exercise your rights, contact us at support@midwestern.construction or call (330) 203-1666.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. We may correct or remove incomplete or inaccurate information at any time.

Cookies

Our Site uses session cookies, persistent cookies, and third-party cookies. Google tracking tools may collect usage data in accordance with Google's terms of service and privacy policy. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Third-Party Information Collection

This policy does not cover the practices of third-party websites or services linked from our Site. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

Data Security

We take great care in implementing and maintaining the security of the Site and your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to or from the Site.

EEA Data Transfers

Data may be transferred outside the European Economic Area only to jurisdictions with adequate protection or with appropriate legal safeguards in place.

Marketing Communications

You may receive promotional materials from us. You can unsubscribe from marketing lists at any time while continuing to receive necessary administrative communications.

Corporate Transactions

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, which will be obligated to honor this Privacy Policy.

Minors

This Site is not designed for children. If you are a minor, parental or guardian consent is required to use this Site. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes take effect immediately upon display on the Site. Continued use of the Site constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.
