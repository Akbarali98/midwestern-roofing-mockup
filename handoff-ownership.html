Overview

Your website runs on three platforms. You need a free account on each one. This guide shows you exactly where to click on each platform.

GitHubStores all the website code
VercelHosts and publishes the website live

Step 1 — GitHub (github.com)

GitHub stores all your website code. Once you create an account, your developer transfers the repository to you.

1
Go to github.com → click Sign Up → enter your email, password, and choose a username → select the Free plan.
2
Share your GitHub username with your developer. They will initiate the transfer. You'll receive an email to accept it.

Step 3 — Sanity (sanity.io)

Sanity is your content editor. You use it to update blog post images without touching any code.

1
Go to sanity.io → click Get Started → sign up with your email.
2
Check your email for an invite from your developer — click Accept Invitation.
3
Your content editor is at: midwestern-roofing.sanity.studio — bookmark this.
