Part 4 — Transfer Sanity Ownership

Once you have a Sanity account, your developer will transfer the project ownership to you. Here's what that looks like from your end.

1
Your developer adds you as an Administrator first — check your email for the Sanity invite and accept it.
2
Once accepted, your developer will go to sanity.io/manage → project settings → transfer ownership to your account.
3
You'll receive an email confirming the transfer. After that, you are the owner of the Sanity project.
